Frequently Asked Questions

View All FAQs
How do I post a new internship opportunity?

To post a new internship opportunity:

  1. Navigate to the "Internship Posts" section
  2. Click on the "New Internship" button
  3. Fill in all the required details about the internship
  4. Specify skills required, duration, and stipend
  5. Submit for verification (takes 24-48 hours)
How long does the verification process take?

The verification process typically takes 24-48 hours. During this time, our team reviews your internship posting to ensure it meets all guidelines and requirements. You will receive an email notification once your internship post is approved and live on the platform.

What are the stipend guidelines for internships?

As per government guidelines:

  • Minimum stipend: ₹8,000 per month for undergraduate interns
  • Minimum stipend: ₹10,000 per month for postgraduate interns
  • Stipends must be paid on or before the 5th of each month
  • Companies must provide internship certificates upon completion
How do I shortlist and select candidates?

To shortlist and select candidates:

  1. Go to the "Applications" section
  2. Filter applications based on your criteria
  3. Review candidate profiles and documents
  4. Use the "Shortlist" button for promising candidates
  5. Schedule interviews using the platform's tools
  6. Update application status after selection
