Program Timeline

12 weeks of intensive, immersive learning and product development

Weeks 1–3: Orientation & Training

Get introduced to the program, build essential skills, and understand project requirements.

Weeks 4–6: Project Initiation

Start working on assigned projects with mentor guidance and apply your learning.

Weeks 7–10: Project Development & Review

Work in-depth on projects, attend mid-program review sessions, and refine your progress.

Weeks 11–12: Final Submission & Certification

Complete project work, present your results, and receive your internship certificate.
